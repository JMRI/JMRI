JMRI Clinics at the NMRA 2008 Anaheim Convention

At the NMRA Anaheim National Convention, July 13th thru July 19th, 2008, there were six JMRI clinics and our traditional annual user meeting was held. Two of the six clinics were repeated.

The slides and JMRI files from David Duchamp's Layout Editor clinic and Dick Bronson's PanelPro clinic are availble on another page.

Several of the clinics were filmed by Model Railroad Hobbyist magazine, and are available from their web site.

The rest of this page is the original schedule announcement from before the meeting.

Our JMRI User Group Meeting will be Wednesday night. The two introductory clinics will be presented on Sunday and repeated on Friday.

The following JMRI clinics have been scheduled:
