Throttle Address Panel

Primary use is to identify the decoders address, and to dispatch or release the identified address. Locomotive address may be entered by one of two methods: typed into text box or use drop-down list to select locomotive from your roster. If you have added Function labels to your Roster, the label text from the Roster will be displayed on the throttle function buttons.
