JMRI: Setting The JMRI Preferences

This Help screen opens automatically when the JMRI Preferences have not been set, or there was a Preferences-related error while the program was starting up.

Once the Preferences are set properly, this screen won't display. The Help pane for setting your Preferences is at Preferences Help (v2.9 and up) or Preferences Help (older). You can also open the help by selecting "Window Help" in the Help menu of the Preferences pane.
