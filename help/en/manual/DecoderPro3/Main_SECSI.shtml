Communications Monitor

Opens the Communications Monitor Window and automatically displays the information that is being sent and received on the communications link between your computer and your DCC system.

Send Command

The Send SECSI serial command prompt opens.

Type the Command into the text box and click the Send button to send the command.

A polling Address: may be typed in the text box at the bottom of the prompt box and click on Send poll to poll the addresses on the communications link.
