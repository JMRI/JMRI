The Advanced Features pane covers CVs that don't fall readily into one of the other categories, and more importantly are not generally part of the NMRA DCC standard. This is a bit like the Netscape/Microsoft Browser Wars, areas where the manufacturer is pushing the envelope. In this case, the CV here controls the Transponding feature of newer Digitrax decoders, which allows them to report their location and engine number back to the LocoNet.

There may be one or several of these panes, depending on how complex the decoder programming is and the style of the person who wrote the definition file for your particular decoder.
