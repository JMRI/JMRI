JMRI: OperationsPro™

JMRI OperationsPro™ allows you to generate train Manifests and switch lists that detail the work a crew will perform during an operations session. JMRI provides extensive documentation on using JMRI to support operating sessions. There are also numerous blogs and videos on the internet about model train operating sessions.

OperationsPro functions can be accessed from the PanelPro main menu by clicking the "Operations" pulldown {prior to JMRI 4.22: Tools⇒Operations}.
