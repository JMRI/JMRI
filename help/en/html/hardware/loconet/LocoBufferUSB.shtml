Hardware Support: Connecting a LocoBuffer-NG or LocoBuffer-USB to LocoNet®

The LocoBuffer-NG or LocoBuffer-USB provide a very reliable way to connect a computer to a LocoNet®. They are produced by Dick Bronson's RR-CirKits company, based in part on the original LocoBuffer design.

Recommended setup

The recommended setup for the LocoBuffer-NG is:

  • Start DecoderPro or other program and configure it for:
    • LocoNet LocoBuffer-NG
    • Select your port

The recommended setup for the LocoBuffer-USB is:

  • Start DecoderPro or other program and configure it for:
    • LocoNet LocoBuffer-USB
    • Select your port

LocoBuffer-USB Power

The LocoBuffer-USB completely isolates your computer from the LocoNet, removing the possibility of ground currents flowing through the LocoNet cable. It receives its power partially from the LocoNet RailSync lines and the rest from your computer via the USB cable.
