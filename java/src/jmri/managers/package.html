Provides implementations of various JMRI managers.

As a long term project, we're moving various Abstract* and Default* managers out of the {@link jmri} package itself, and moved into here. In the meantime, you'll find many of them still in {@link jmri}.

Implementations of individual object interfaces are found in the {@link jmri.implementation} package. @see jmri.implementation @since 2.5.3
