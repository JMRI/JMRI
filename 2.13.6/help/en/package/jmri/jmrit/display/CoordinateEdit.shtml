The Label and Icon Property Editor

This editor allows you modify the properties of the elements on your panel.

Setting x and y Coordinates

There are two text boxes where you can optionally enter the x and y coordinates that you desire and by pressing the "Set" button the icon will move to that location. If you wish to return the icon to its original location when the editor was opened, press the "Cancel" button and the editor will move the icon back to its original position and close the editor window.

Use the spinner if you want to move the icon one pixel.

Changing the display Level

Enter the desired display level in the text box. The levels range from 1 to 10, with 10 being the top layer. Use the level setting to place one icon above another to get the view you want on your panel.

Changing the Tooltip Text

Enter the text you want to display as a tooltip for the panel item.

Setting the Size of the Border

Enter the size in pixels for the border width.

Setting the Size of the Margin

Enter the width and height for the Margin.

Setting the Fixed Size

Enter the width and height for a fixed region to contain the item.

Rotating an Icon

Enter the angle in degrees to rotate the icon an additional amount from its present position. To restore the icon to its original angle, enter the value 0. To restore the original angle, size and position, click on the Rotate (Orthogonal) item.

Scaling an Icon

Enter the percentage to increase or decrease the size of the icon an additional amount from its present size. To restore the icon to its original size, enter the value 100. To restore the original angle, size and position, click on the Rotate (Orthogonal) item.

Changing the Text

Enter the desired text in the text box.
