JMRI decoder Comma-Separated-Variables files

  • decodersizes.csv Physical size for each defined decoder
  • decoderpower.csv Power limits and connector by decoder
  • cvsummary.csv CVs present in each decoder - this is limited by the CSV format, and doesn't include all the information in the JMRI decoder definitions
  • reservedCVs.csv NMRA reserved CVs present in each decoder
Many people have worked on this, and we thank all of them. If you find this information useful, we encourage you to donate to support JMRI.
