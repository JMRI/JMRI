JMRI Decoder Comma-Separated Values files

  • decodersizes.csv   Physical size for each defined decoder
  • decoderpower.csv   Power limits and connector by decoder
  • cvsummary.csv   CVs present in each decoder - this is limited by the CSV file format, and doesn't include all the information in the JMRI decoder definitions
  • reservedCVs.csv   NMRA reserved CVs present in each decoder

Right-click on one of the underlined hyperlinks and choose "Download linked file" to save a local copy.

International users using a comma as the decimal separator may want to replace the comma used in these files as the field separator by a semicolon ";" and then replace the decimal separator period "." by a comma to process these files in their localized spreadsheet application.
