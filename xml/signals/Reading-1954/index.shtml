JMRI: Reading 1954 Signaling Definition

This directory contains signaling definitions corresponding to the Reading 1954 rules as described in the publication "Reading Company Rules for the Government of the Operating Department Effective January 1, 1954"".

See the aspect page.

Signal Mast definitions:

  • Triple searchlight high signal
  • Triple searchlight high signal (alternate)
  • Double searchlight high absolute signal
  • Double searchlight high permissive signal
  • Double searchlight high permissive signal (alternate)
  • Double searchlight dwarf signal
  • High over low searchlight signal
  • Single searchlight high signal
  • Single searchlight dwarf signal
