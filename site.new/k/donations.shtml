JMRI Donations

JMRI runs entirely on volunteer labor. We have an impressive list of contributors in that respect.

Unfortunately, Matt Katzer has forced us to spend a lot of money for trademark and copyright registrations, legal filing fees, court reporters, copies of official documents, etc.

Patent, trademark and copyright litigation is expensive, and the only way we have to offset those costs is to ask for donations.

Please donate -- $5, $10, $25, $50, $100 or more -- to help us in this fight by clicking on the button to the right. The SourceForge/PayPal system completely shields your identity unless you choose to provide it, and we keep no records of who has donated (though we're grateful for every one, we don't want to risk Katzer demanding the records and annoying even more model railroaders and Open Source proponents)
